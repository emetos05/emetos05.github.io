My Projects

Here are some of the projects I worked on during my study at the University of Regina.


Attack detection in Internet of Things

The algorithm developed in this project detects a change in the network traffic pattern in an IoT monitoring system and notifies the user. C programming language was used to implement the software, while Arduino©, Xbee© and NXP© chips were used for the hardware implementation.

Bash scripting for digital protection relay

In this project, I implemented a software based digital protective relay for electrical power system using bash scripting. The program utilized both current and voltage magnitudes to determine abnormality in the power system. This software is typical of those utilized in commercial protective relays.
